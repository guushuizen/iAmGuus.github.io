About

Hi there, I am Guus, a 13 year old boy from Rotterdam, Netherlands. I have been developing since I was 9 years old by making little Habbo Hotels and editing the CMSes. From then on I also started learning Java to create Android apps (which I am still learning) and also to create Minecraft Server Plugins. I started very easy by just creating simple console applications to in the end creating rather difficult apps/plugins. Throughout my time learning Java and the Bukkit API, I started creating also rather easy plugins and ending with rather difficult plugins like minigames and complex core plugins et cetera. Just now I have opened up my own shop on Spigot and MC-Market. Thanks for reading through my portfolio and I hope to do business with you soon.
